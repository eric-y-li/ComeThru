What is ComeThru?

ComeThru is a website designed for you to find an event near you when you are feeling spontaneous and looking for something to do.
Because we want to disrupt the systems that structure our lives, each event will only be visible for 24 hours before it is deleted.
By doing this, we want to ensure that you don't have to fret about planning or other boring stuff.
All you have to do is pick an event that sounds fun and ComeThru!
